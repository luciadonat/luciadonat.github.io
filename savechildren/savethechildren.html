Teddy bears don’t work

Build-A-Bear and Save the Children join forces to expose the stark realities of child labor.

Through a series of thought-provoking visuals, teddy bears are portrayed performing different forms of work — a poignant metaphor for the millions of children that are being deprived of play, education, and safety. The campaign is designed not only to raise awareness but also to ignite dialogue and inspire meaningful action. By inviting audiences to support protective policies, contribute to initiatives, and advocate for systemic change, we aim to advance a world where childhood is never sacrificed for survival.

Because teddy bears don't work, they belong to playgrounds as children should.
