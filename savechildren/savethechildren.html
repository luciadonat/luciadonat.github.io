Teddy bears don’t work

Build-A-Bear and Save the Children join forces to raise awareness and inspire meaningful action against child labor.

In this campaign, teddy bears come to life through AI-generated videos that show them performing different types of labor. A metaphor that mirrors the reality of more than 138 million children worldwide who are forced to work from a very young age. These children are deprived of their childhood and exposed to extremely harsh conditions that not only deny them the right to play, but also destroy their chance of a dignified future.

Teddy bears belong in playgrounds just as children should.
