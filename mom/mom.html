Mom, I'am the rich man

My mother, a professional photographer, raised me surrounded by photography books. Among them was one of Taschen’s monumental volumes dedicated to the work of Helmut Newton.

This project is my personal tribute to Rue Aubriot, Yves Saint Laurent, Vogue France, 1975 a photograph were the subject, in this case the actress Vibeke Knudsen is depicted as sophisticated yet powerful. Strength that were well-captured Cher’s now-iconic words in her 1996 interview with Jane Pauley “Mom, I am the rich man.”
